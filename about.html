HELLO!

I'm Lan, a videographer and photographer based out of Chicago and Northwest Indiana. I'm currently freelancing as well as pursuing my personal endeavors: Lan Franczek Photography.

Visualization is my outlet for creative expression. Whether it's through video production or photography, I enjoy telling stories in the medium I know best. I see natural beauty in my surroundings and aspire towards producing art that incorporates my greatest passions: music, travel, animals and people.

My goal is to bring adventure, insightful and inspirational imagery to others. In doing this, I'll make my mark in the world, one day at a time.
